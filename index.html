Himani Ananthula More information but within the line-size limit.

I am a PhD Candidate in Operations Management at the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University, advised by Professor. Achal Bassamboo and Professor. Milind Sohoni. Before pursuing my PhD, I completed my undergraduate studies at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, in 2016, where I majored in Mechanical Engineering and minored in Electrical Engineering.

My research examines labor supply dynamics in the gig economy and develops optimal strategies for service providers managing flexible workforce. I employ data-driven analytical modeling and econometric models in my work.
